Take Action

In order for a change to occur, we need to make it happen. Even if you can’t do everything, even a small effort can make a difference. Luckily, there are many ways you can contribute!

Action Item List

Once you complete something on the list, click on it to check it off your list! You can refer to the resource page for more information.

  • Read more information and articles to learn more
  • Stay updated by subscribing to emails or texts
  • Educate yourself on how to deal with these issues
  • Do research on your political representation
  • Prioritize your health and disease prevention
  • Take care of yourself and support others
  • Sign petitions
  • Support organizations that support similar causes
  • Volunteer (online or in person)
  • Donate
  • Attend events/protests
