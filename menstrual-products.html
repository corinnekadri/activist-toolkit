Why are there taxes?

There are taxes in many states on menstrual products such as pads and tampons, as if they are unessential products. However, these products are vital for women to live a healthy and comfortable lifestyle, and are thus, completely essential.

What should change?

Many say that products such as pads and tampons should be covered in basic healthcare plans for women. Others argue that this is not a plausible change, and that prices should simply be lowered, or that there should not be taxes for menstrual products. However, it is important to note that many other kinds of expensive medications are fully or partially covered by certain healthcare plans. This leaves no doubt that such measures can be taken to support women and provide sanitary and mesntrual needs.
